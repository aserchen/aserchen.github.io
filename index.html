Introduction

Hi, I'm Yasong Chen, a full-time teacher majoring in software engineering at Hanjiang Normal University. Prior to that, I worked as an algorithm application engineer at DaHuaTech.

I graduated from Central China Normal University(CCNU) with a Bachelor's and a Master's degree, under the supervision of Associate Professor Chen Jia. Recently, my research interest lies at computer vision, and focuses on image/video segmentation specifically.
